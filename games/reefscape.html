The Game

(in great detail)

"In REEFSCAPE presented by Haas, FIRST® Robotics Competition teams will use their engineering skills to strengthen one of the ocean’s most diverse habitats." - firstinspires.org
Autonomous Period:

During the first 15 seconds of the match, autonomous robots navigate the arena based on pre-programmed routines. Exiting the "Starting Zone" earns the team 2 points. Scoring in this phase is awarded as follows: 2 points for a low-level goal and 5 points for a high-level goal.

Tele-op:

In the 2:15 tele-op period, drivers score coral on reef levels and collect algae for the processor or barge net. If both alliances place two algae in their processors, everyone earns a Coopertition Point toward an extra ranking point.

Endgame:

In the final part of the match, robots can climb one of their alliance’s three cages. Each cage can be shallow (easier) or deep (harder). Hanging on a shallow cage scores 6 points, while climbing a deep cage scores 12 points.
