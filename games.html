Crescendo - 2024

Crescendo, the 2024 FIRST Robotics Competition game, had a music theme. In this game, two alliances, each with three teams, compete by completing various tasks. These tasks include shooting rings, referred to as Notes, into goals and hanging on chains at the end of the match.
